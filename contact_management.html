About HALO

As the world’s largest music and audio platform, SoundCloud lets people discover and enjoy the greatest selection of music from the most diverse creator community on earth. Since launching in 2008, the platform has become renowned for its unique content and features, including the ability to share music and connect directly with artists, as well as unearth breakthrough tracks, raw demos, podcasts and more. This is made possible by an open platform that directly connects creators and their fans across the globe. Music and audio creators use SoundCloud to both share and monetise their content with a global audience, as well as receive detailed stats and feedback from the SoundCloud community.

Community Support

If you need help, or have questions about your account, find out how you can get the support you need below:

Help Center. Use our Help Center to find answers to common questions, understand how to get the most out of using SoundCloud and to contact our support team.

Help Community. Join the conversation and ask the community for help when you need it. The Help Community is a place for you to interact with SoundCloud and its community.

Copyright Claim Center. Here you can find and dispute any copyright claims filed by third parties related to your uploads.

Reporting Copyright Infringement. If you identify content on SoundCloud that you believe infringes your copyright, you can let us know here.

@scsupport. Need help fast? Tweet our community support team with your problem, and we’ll get it sorted for you as soon as we can.
